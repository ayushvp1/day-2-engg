Career and Placement Guide

Resume Tips:
- Keep your resume concise (1-2 pages).
- Highlight relevant skills and projects.
- Use action verbs: Developed, Implemented, Designed, Optimized.
- Quantify achievements where possible.

Interview Preparation:
- Practice common technical and behavioral questions.
- Use the STAR method for behavioral answers (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
- Research the company thoroughly before the interview.
- Prepare questions to ask the interviewer.

In-Demand Skills for 2025:
- Python, JavaScript, TypeScript
- Machine Learning & AI
- Cloud Computing (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- DevOps & CI/CD
- System Design & Architecture
