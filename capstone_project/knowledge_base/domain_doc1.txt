Academic AI Assistant - Study Guide

Key Concepts in Computer Science:

Data Structures: Arrays, Linked Lists, Stacks, Queues, Trees, Graphs, Hash Tables.
Algorithms: Sorting (Merge Sort, Quick Sort), Searching (Binary Search), Dynamic Programming.
Object-Oriented Programming: Encapsulation, Inheritance, Polymorphism, Abstraction.
Databases: SQL, NoSQL, Normalization, Indexing, Transactions.
Networking: TCP/IP, HTTP, DNS, REST APIs, WebSockets.

Study Tips:
- Practice coding problems daily on platforms like LeetCode or HackerRank.
- Build mini-projects to solidify concepts.
- Review past exam papers and solve them under timed conditions.
- Form study groups for peer learning and discussion.
